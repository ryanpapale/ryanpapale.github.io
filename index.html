About Me

I grew up in Oceanside, CA, and in 2025 I received my PhD in Economics from the University of Bristol. My research focused on applied labor economics, particularly in education.

In my spare time I enjoy watching baseball and running regressions in R.

This website is where I will share my personal projects, mostly in baseball and economics, but possibly other things I find interesting as well.

You can find my baseball projects in the Baseball section. In the Economics section I have uploaded some of my research. And my contact details are in the Contact section.
